Features

Designed for Student Success

Leveraging machine learning to map academic metrics directly to high-impact campus events.

Personalized Recommendations

Recommends ideal events by evaluating year, academic attendance, feedback behavior, and engagement levels.

AI-Based Matching

Uses a Random Forest Classifier trained on student historical participation logs to achieve highly accurate predictions.

Department Specific Suggestions

Student Engagement Analytics

Encourages higher engagement by recommending events that match and challenge your current skill progress.
